Welcome to the world of Aatma-ta!

This is the hub page for everything you need to know about the world of our Dungegons and Dragons campaign. This website is a compendium of all the lore of the world thus far devised. Here you can learn about the world your charachter lives in and thus be better able to play that charachter.

The site also features recources for d&d players in general. Here you can download all of the (non module) books, as well as charachter sheet pdfs.

You can also get more involved with the world. It's a big project, and we're always open to contributions! Head over to the activities page to fing out more.
